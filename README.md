Solus — the sound the sun would make if it sang.
Everything is gold on deep void-black. The logo is a chrome gradient that sweeps white-hot through bright gold to deep amber. The atmosphere particles are drifting solar wind motes ranging from pale yellow to deep burnt orange, and they accelerate and multiply when you're playing.
The solar disc is a live canvas animation in the top left — a radial gradient sun with a bright white-hot core fading through gold to amber edge, three animated solar flare arcs that rotate around the surface and stretch outward as audio energy increases, surface granulation texture that appears when you're playing, and four layered corona rings that expand and brighten with the sound. The whole thing responds to the audio in real time.
The sawtooth is built from scratch — 16 individual sine oscillators at integer multiples of the fundamental, each one contributing its harmonic partial. This means you can shape the harmonic spectrum directly: the Odd knob controls how much power goes into the 1st, 3rd, 5th, 7th harmonics and the Even knob controls the 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th. Every partial has its own slow shimmer LFO at a slightly different rate, so the sound is always in gentle motion. The 8 harmonic bars in the header show the current spectrum shape in real time and update when you switch characters or adjust the knobs.
Five characters:

Solar Saw — the pure harmonic series, warm and full
Plasma — three detuned sawtooths through a waveshaper, overdriven and seething
Corona — emphasizes the high partials (8x-16x), thin and shimmering like the outer ring
Forge — square plus fat saw, waveshaped with a power function for heavy industrial weight
Ancient — five layers with extremely slow drift LFOs, breathing at 0.03Hz, as if the sound has been going since before anything else

Solar Flare on the attack knob adds a bright noise burst bandpassed to the 6th harmonic — that initial transient that cuts through before the pad blooms. Fold runs the output through a wavefolder that creates new harmonic structure. Heat controls filter brightness quadratically so it has almost no effect at the low end but opens dramatically at the top. Mass adds resonance, thickening the filter peak.
The frequency spectrum visualizer fills the filter display with a golden-to-amber gradient that shows you exactly what's happening spectrally. The flare strip at top is a fine waveform in a gradient from pale cream through gold to burnt orange.
